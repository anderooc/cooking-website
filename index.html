What is steak?

When focusing on beef, steak is:
A slice of meat cut from the fleshy part of a beef carcass.
Beef steaks are generally cut across the muscle fiber of a
large section of beef and may or may not include a bone.
Most steaks come from three prime areas on a cow.

Ingredients

Ingredients Amount
Ribeye or New York Steaks 1-2 steaks
Oil of your choice(I use avocado oil) Enough to spread over the entire pan
Butter 2 tablespoon (largely personal preference)
Salt 1 tablespoon (depends on size and type of beef)
Pepper 1 tablespoon (depends on size and type of beef)
(Optional) Garlic 2-3 cloves
(Optional) Thyme A few sprigs

Instructions

  1. Take steaks out of fridge
  2. If steak is still wet, wipe down with paper towel
  3. Salt and pepper steak
    1. Rub salt and pepper into steak
  4. Turn on pan and pour oil
    1. Spread oil over entire pan (i.e. with a spatula)
  5. Put steak in once pot is hot enough (steak should shizzle)
    1. If steak does not sizzle, either the pan is not hot enough, or there is still water
  6. Flip steak every 1-1.5 minutes
  7. Make sure to keep measuring the temperature of the steak with a thermometer. Take steak out once it reaches the temperature you want
  8. Let steak cool and serve

Optimal Steps for Optimal Steak

  1. Buy A-5 wagyu beef
  2. Tell steak positive affirmations
  3. Salt steak the night before and refrigerate
